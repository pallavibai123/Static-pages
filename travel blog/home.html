Exploring Paris

Paris was absolutely stunning! From the Eiffel Tower to the cozy cafés, every corner had its own charm. This trip was filled with art, croissants, and breathtaking views.

Chilling in Bali

Bali was all about beaches, sunsets, and relaxation. I enjoyed the tropical vibes and friendly locals. Don’t miss the waterfalls and island hopping!

New York Adventures

The city that never sleeps! I walked through Times Square, ate the best street food, and saw an amazing Broadway show. NYC is energy and culture all in one place.
